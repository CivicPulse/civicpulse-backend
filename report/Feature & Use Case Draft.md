
Civic App – Feature & Use Case Draft

  

  

  

Overview

  

  

A multi-tenant CRM + CMS system tailored for nonprofits, civic organizations, and political groups. The platform centralizes outreach, governance transparency, election tracking, and volunteer coordination. Designed with security, usability, and accessibility as top priorities.

  

  

  

  

Core Features

  

  

  

1. Contact & Outreach Management

  

  

- Track calls, emails, and visits to constituents, donors, or officials.
- Log outcomes and schedule follow-ups.
- Assign contacts or tasks to team members.
- Voice note input with AI transcription for quick field reporting.

  

  

  

2. Elected Officials & Voting Records

  

  

- Maintain profiles of elected officials.
- Record stances on issues and voting history.
- Searchable, filterable database.
- Public-facing transparency mode (optional).

  

  

  

3. Election Tracking

  

  

- Calendar of upcoming elections (local, state, federal).
- Candidate directory with qualifications and districts.
- Voter information lookups tied to districts.

  

  

  

4. Governance Directory

  

  

- Comprehensive list of local boards, commissions, and authorities.
- Includes roles, responsibilities, meeting times, and contacts.
- Integrated meeting calendar with reminders for coverage.

  

  

  

5. AI-Powered Assistance

  

  

- Natural language Q&A: “Who covers zoning issues?”
- Automated summarization of meeting minutes.
- Smart search across officials, boards, and elections.

  

  

  

6. GIS & Mapping Integration

  

  

- Interactive maps of districts, precincts, facilities, etc.
- Layered data views (e.g., officials by district).
- Connects geography to people, elections, and organizations.

  

  

  

7. Multi-Tenant Structure

  

  

- Separate spaces for different organizations.
- Shared access to public data layers (e.g., voting precincts).
- Role-based permissions for fine-grained control.

  

  

  

8. User Experience

  

  

- Dual-mode UI:  
    

- Simple mode for less tech-savvy users.
- Advanced mode for power users.

-   
    
- Fully mobile-friendly design.

  

  

  

9. Volunteer Safety & Field Tools

  

  

- Periodic volunteer location logging for safety.
- Admin visibility of last-known check-in.
- Quick task/route assignment.

  

  

  

  

  

Future Considerations

  
- API endpoints for third-party civic apps.
- Data privacy compliance (GDPR/CCPA where applicable).
- Offline-first mode for rural canvassing.
