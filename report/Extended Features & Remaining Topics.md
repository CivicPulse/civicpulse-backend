## Overview
This document captures additional features, enhancements, and remaining topics from brainstorming sessions. It complements the **Feature Draft** and serves as a roadmap for deeper exploration.

---

## Extended Features

### 1. Volunteer Recognition & Badges
- Digital badges or recognition for volunteers.  
- Earned through tasks, attendance, or campaigns.  
- Option for **end-of-year recognition ceremonies**.  
- Gamification elements (leaderboards, challenges).  
- Private tracking until officially revealed.  

### 2. Public-Facing Transparency Portal
- CMS-style pages for news, reports, and updates.  
- Organizations choose what to make public.  
- Clear publishing workflow to avoid accidental disclosures.  
- Optional review/approval process before publishing.  
- Builds public trust while protecting sensitive info.  

### 3. Integrated Messaging & Announcements
- In-app announcement banners (color-coded, optional dismiss).  
- Critical messages with **acknowledgment tracking**.  
- Multi-channel notifications (email, SMS, push).  
- Distinct from newsletters (short, actionable messages only).  

### 4. Training Modules
- Section for onboarding guides, volunteer manuals, and training materials.  
- Can support text, video, or downloadable files.  
- Tracks completion status for compliance or readiness.  

### 5. Community Forums & Discussion Boards
- Optional space for member discussion and idea-sharing.  
- Moderation tools for admins.  
- Can be limited to specific roles or open to all members.  
- Encourages collaboration outside formal meetings.
